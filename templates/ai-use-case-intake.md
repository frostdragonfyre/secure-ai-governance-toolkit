# AI Use Case Intake

This intake form supports the early review of proposed AI-enabled systems, tools, workflows, or vendor capabilities. It should be completed before procurement, implementation, production deployment, or broad internal use.

The purpose of this document is to establish a clear record of the business purpose, system ownership, data exposure, operational dependency, risk considerations, and required review path for the proposed use case.

## 12. Initial Risk Tier

The initial risk tier should be based on the intended use, data sensitivity, degree of automation, external impact, operational dependency, and potential harm. A low-risk use case generally involves limited internal use, low-sensitivity data, no material impact on individuals or operations, and required human review. A moderate-risk use case generally involves sensitive business processes, moderate data sensitivity, limited external impact, or meaningful reliance on AI-generated output. A high-risk use case generally involves regulated data, high-impact decisions, significant operational dependency, external users, privileged system access, or potential legal, financial, security, or reputational harm. A use case may be withheld from approval when the risk is unacceptable without additional controls, executive approval, legal review, or material redesign.
